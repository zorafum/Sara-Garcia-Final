How I Created This Video

I wanted to show what my Erasmus experience has felt like, using ID photos that I’ve been collecting along the way. Some of them I brought from home, and others I got here from new friends I’ve met.

The idea was to represent who stayed, who left, and how my friend group has changed over time. The order of the photos kind of reflects the order I met people, too.

I used different effects to help tell that story: coloring filters, fade-ins and fade-outs, and even things like grain and paint-bucket style effects. The whole video is in stop motion, which I thought matched the personal, handmade feeling I wanted.
